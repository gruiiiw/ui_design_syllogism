BUILDING BLOCK 2: PROPOSITIONS

A proposition involves connecting two terms, a subject and a predicate, sometimes using a copula, or a connector, such as “is,” “are,” “was,” or “were.”

Propositions take the form of a declarative sentence and can always be true or false

Example: “I am hungry” is a proposition; “Feed me!” is not.
